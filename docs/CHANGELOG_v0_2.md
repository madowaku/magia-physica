# CHANGELOG v0.2

## Added

- Run state and battle sequence.
- Actual deck, draw pile, discard pile, hand cards.
- Formula power investment buttons from 1 to 5.
- Card preview based on selected card and investment value.
- Reward cards after each battle.
- Four enemy battles.
- Basic enemy actions.
- Overload penalty when investing 5 formula power in variable-cost cards.

## Card effects

- Push Formula: push enemy, wall collision damage.
- Momentum Needle: direct damage, consumes pebble for bonus.
- Heat Rune: damage and burn.
- Slip Glyph: adds next-push bonus.
- Mass Scan: draw 1, next mechanics formula bonus.
- Margin Recovery: formula power +2, draw 1.
- Pebble Create: pebble +2, draw 1.
